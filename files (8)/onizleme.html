05
KurulumHTML kodu · Dosya listesi

İkonları /public/icons/ klasörüne (Next.js) veya /static/icons/ (diğer framework'ler) kopyala. Ayrıca favicon.ico'yu sitenin köküne (/favicon.ico) da koy — bazı eski botlar orayı arar.

<!-- Favicon — modern tarayıcılar SVG'yi tercih eder, dark mode otomatik -->
<link rel="icon" type="image/svg+xml" href="/icons/favicon.svg">

<!-- PNG fallback -->
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="32x32" href="/icons/favicon-32.png">
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="16x16" href="/icons/favicon-16.png">

<!-- Legacy .ico -->
<link rel="shortcut icon" href="/favicon.ico">

<!-- Apple Touch Icon · iOS ana ekran -->
<link rel="apple-touch-icon" sizes="180x180" href="/icons/apple-touch-icon.png">

<!-- PWA manifest -->
<link rel="manifest" href="/icons/site.webmanifest">

<!-- Tema renkleri -->
<meta name="theme-color" content="#0D5C4A">
<meta name="msapplication-TileColor" content="#0D5C4A">
<meta name="apple-mobile-web-app-title" content="Ekosistem">
